Kronk's Spinach Puffs

Description

The spinach puffs are a famous recipe by Kronk. They consist of a simple cooked dough filled with spinach.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups frozen spinach
  • ½ cup ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup feta cheese
  • 1 egg yolk
  • ½ teaspoon salt/li>
  • Frozen puff pastry
  • Egg wash

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400ºF.
  2. Heat oil in a sauté pan on medium high. Add diced onions and sweat for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and spinach and cook for another 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Place spinach into a medium bowl and then stir in the cheeses, yolk and salt.
  5. Cut a puff pastry into 3 to 4 inch squares and spread 1 tablespoon of filling in a line along the center.
  6. Brush egg wash along the edges and then seal the two sides together to create a “cone” shape.
  7. Bake for 15 to 17 minutes.
